Deb Hindman Drops Out of Comal County Judge Race

February 14, 2026 By Hill Country Sentinel Staff

Deb Hindman withdrew from the Comal County Judge Republican primary, leaving Kristen Hoyt and Kayne Parrish as remaining candidates. Her husband announced the withdrawal on Nextdoor during a community meeting about the Broken Cedar Ranch development.
