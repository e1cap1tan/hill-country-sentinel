# Local business owner, veteran Jon Frazier announces campaign for New Braunfels Mayor **Date:** Feb 28, 2026 **Source:** New Braunfels Herald-Zeitung Jon Frazier, a disabled veteran, local small business owner and community volunteer, has announced that he is running for mayor of New Braunfels in the May 2, 2026, municipal election. Frazier said his campaign will focus on transparency in city government, responsible growth planning, infrastructure improvements and protecting the long-term affordability of New Braunfels. The mayoral election is scheduled for May 2, 2026. Early voting information will be announced by local election officials.
