Michael French Draws 45 to First Mayoral Town Hall

February 14, 2026 By Hill Country Sentinel Staff

Mayoral candidate Michael French drew 45 residents to his first campaign town hall at Coopers Pit Bar-B-Que, fielding questions about city issues and his vision for New Braunfels. French is one of five candidates in the mayoral race, with early voting beginning February 17.
