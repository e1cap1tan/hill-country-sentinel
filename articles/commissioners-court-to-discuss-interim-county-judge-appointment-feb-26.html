Commissioners Court to Discuss Interim County Judge Appointment Feb 26

February 14, 2026 By Hill Country Sentinel Staff

Comal County Commissioners Court will discuss appointing an interim county judge on February 26, following Judge Sherman Krauses death. The appointment ensures continuity while three Republican candidates compete in the March primary for the full term.
