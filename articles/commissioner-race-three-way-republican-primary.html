Comal County Commissioner Race Heats Up with Three-Way Republican Primary

February 14, 2026 By Hill Country Sentinel Staff

Three candidates have filed to run for Comal County Commissioner Precinct 2 in the upcoming Republican primary election. The race features incumbent Sarah Martinez facing challenges from local business owner Tom Rodriguez and former school board member Jennifer Walsh. The primary election is scheduled for March 5, 2026, with early voting beginning February 14.

The three-way Republican primary race for Commissioner Precinct 2 brings together diverse professional backgrounds. Incumbent Sarah Martinez seeks a second term after focusing on infrastructure improvements during her first term. Tom Rodriguez brings private sector experience from the construction industry, while Jennifer Walsh leverages her education background from school board service.

Key issues in the race include road maintenance, water infrastructure, and managing development growth as the county continues to expand rapidly. All three candidates are expected to participate in upcoming candidate forums before early voting begins.
