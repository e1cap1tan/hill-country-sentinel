Comal County Commissioner Race Heats Up with Three-Way Republican Primary

February 14, 2026 By Hill Country Sentinel Staff

Three candidates have filed to run for Comal County Commissioner Precinct 2 in the upcoming Republican primary election. The race features incumbent Sarah Martinez facing challenges from local business owner Tom Rodriguez and former school board member Jennifer Walsh. The primary election is scheduled for March 5, 2026, with early voting beginning February 14.
