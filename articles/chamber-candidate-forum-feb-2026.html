New Braunfels Chamber Hosts Pre-Primary Candidate Forum

February 12, 2026 By Hill Country Sentinel Staff

The New Braunfels Chamber of Commerce hosted a candidate forum Wednesday night ahead of the March primary election, providing voters a chance to hear from candidates seeking office in both Comal and Guadalupe counties.

According to reports from the event, all commissioners court candidates for both counties were Republican, reflecting the heavily conservative makeup of the region's primary contests.

The forum featured candidates for multiple offices, including those seeking the Comal County Judge position, which became vacant following the death of Judge Sherman Krause on February 7, 2026.

Three Republican candidates are vying for the county judge position in the March primary: former Comal County Tax Assessor-Collector Kristen Hoyt, business owner Deb Hindman, and local firefighter Kayne Parrish.

The event comes at a critical time with early voting beginning February 17 and election day scheduled for March 3, 2026.
