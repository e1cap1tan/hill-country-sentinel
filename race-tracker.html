How We Rate Races

Our assessments are based on publicly available information and transparent criteria:

  • Incumbency — Incumbents win 80-90% of local races nationally
  • Endorsements — Key organizational backing (Chamber, party officials, PACs)
  • Campaign Finance — Money raised and donor count (when filings are available)
  • Historical Patterns — Past results in the same district/seat
  • Campaign Activity — Town halls, grassroots engagement, media presence
  • Prediction Markets — Live betting odds from Polymarket where available

These are informed assessments, not predictions. Local races are inherently volatile — a single event can shift the outcome. We present the evidence and let you decide.
