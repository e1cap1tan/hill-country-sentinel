# Hill Country Sentinel — Vision & Mission

*Last updated: May 22, 2026*

## Mission Statement

The Hill Country Sentinel is a resource for conservatives and people with traditional values in the New Braunfels / Comal County area.

**Tagline:** Informed Voters. Accountable Leaders.

## Priorities (in order)

### Priority 1: Election Readiness — Informed Voters

Keep voters as informed as possible on upcoming elections and what's at stake.

- **Candidate Research:** Extensive, thorough profiles on every candidate — voting records, donors, endorsements, public statements, positions on key issues. Voters should have everything they need to make an informed decision.
- **Election Awareness:** Election dates front and center. No one should miss an election because they didn't know about it.
- **Voter Tools:** Make voting as easy as possible:
  - Find your polling location (Find My Officials tool)
  - Voter registration links and deadlines
  - Early voting dates and locations
  - Election day info
  - Sample ballots when available
  - "Know Before You Go" voter guides per election cycle

### Priority 2: Accountability & Watchdog

Keep conservative residents informed on potentially concerning activity in our community.

- **Elected Official Accountability:** Track votes by elected representatives that go against conservative values. Scorecard format — simple, clear, shareable.
- **Local Business & Government Watch:** Monitor activity by local businesses or government entities that conflicts with traditional values (DEI initiatives, political stances, policy decisions).

## Editorial Lens

Conservative values guide our coverage:
- Limited government
- Low taxes
- Strong borders
- Parental rights
- Property rights
- Traditional values
- Government transparency & accountability

## What We Are NOT

- Not a general news site — we cover politics and civic life
- Not a gossip outlet — everything sourced and factual
- Not partisan hit pieces — we present facts and let readers decide, but we don't pretend to be neutral. We have a perspective.
